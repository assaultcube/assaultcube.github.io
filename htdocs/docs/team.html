About

AssaultCube Version 1.3

AssaultCube has been created and nurtured by an international community of artists and developers since July 2004.

We are people who love building fun games.

We are looking for lead developers and lead artists to help us build the next generation of AssaultCube for Windows, Mac, Linux and Mobile.
